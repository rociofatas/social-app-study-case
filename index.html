Designing a social app for meaningful connections

Mingling was a startup project focused on creating deeper, more intentional connections in the digital space. In 2021, I joined the design team as a UX/UI designer, helping shape the app’s identity, flows, and interface system. My work spanned research, concepting, and building a design library that could scale with future features.

Project Overview

The goal of Mingling was to challenge the superficiality of traditional social platforms by fostering genuine connections. The founders needed a user experience that felt fresh yet intuitive, and an interface that balanced playfulness with clarity. I contributed by building visual foundations, creating modular components, and refining flows for mobile and web touchpoints.

Goals & Challenges

  • Design an interface that encourages authentic social interactions
  • Establish a consistent visual system to support evolving features
  • Create UX flows that feel natural for first-time users
  • Balance an innovative aesthetic with accessibility and clarity

My Role

As a UX/UI designer embedded in a small startup team, I:

  • Designed core screens for the app: onboarding, profile, and matching flows
  • Built a modular UI library in Figma, with components ready for handoff
  • Worked closely with developers to ensure design feasibility
  • Helped craft the visual identity, from iconography to color palette exploration

Process Highlights

1. Research & Discovery

We benchmarked other social platforms, interviewed potential users, and mapped pain points in existing digital interactions.

2. Design System Creation

I developed a component-based design system in Figma, documenting typography, buttons, and layouts to ensure consistency across the app.

3. UI Design & Iteration

From wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes, I designed and tested screens, iterating based on feedback from users and the startup team.

Results & Impact

The app’s interface established a strong design foundation for development. Although the startup later pivoted, the system we created became a reusable blueprint for similar projects.

Key Outcomes:

  • Created a scalable design library for the startup’s core product
  • Improved UX clarity and visual coherence across the app
  • Delivered wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes for key flows
  • Set a visual tone that combined freshness, warmth, and usability

Conclusions

Mingling represents my ability to work on early-stage products, translating a concept into a tangible design system. It’s a project that combined creativity, research, and collaboration—essential elements in any digital design challenge.
